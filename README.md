## Why this exists

There are thousands of Pakistani students trying to break into data science. Most of them don't know where to start, can't afford paid courses, and have no one to guide them.

There was no single place on the internet that had everything in one spot ‚Äî free courses in Urdu and English, Pakistani datasets, local communities, job boards, roadmaps.

**This is that place. And it's built by us, for us.**

## Jobs & Freelancing

### Local Jobs
- [Rozee.pk](https://www.rozee.pk/) ‚Äî search "data analyst", "data scientist", "Python developer"
- [Mustakbil.com](https://www.mustakbil.com/) ‚Äî another Pakistani job board
- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/) ‚Äî many Pakistani companies hire through here

### Freelancing (earn in dollars)
- [Upwork](https://www.upwork.com/) ‚Äî data analysis, ML models, dashboards
- [Fiverr](https://www.fiverr.com/) ‚Äî data cleaning, visualization gigs
- [Toptal](https://www.toptal.com/) ‚Äî harder to get in but pays the most

### What skills make you hireable fast
```
SQL          ‚Üí  every data job needs this
Excel        ‚Üí  still used everywhere
Python       ‚Üí  pandas + matplotlib minimum
Tableau/PowerBI ‚Üí  visualization tools companies love
```
